Curriculum

Through the guidance of the school’s motto; ‘Elimu ni Ushindi’, the students are able to cope with a broad curriculum. This is also meant to ensure a smooth and harmonious pursuit of our goals. Nyeri High School offers a broad curriculum comprising of the following subject cluster:-

Compulsory

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Kiswahili

Sciences

  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

Humanities

  • History/Government
  • Geography
  • C.R.E.

Group IV

  • Agriculture
  • Computer Studies

Group V

  • Business Studies
  • German
  • French

Clear and Focused Mission

At Nyeri High School, we are committed to our mission to prepare all students with a strong foundation of skills and knowledge to succeed in their educational pursuits. Further, we work toward the realization of our district’s vision and providing interventions where indicated to improve student learning and achievement.

Who We Are...

Kigumo Bendera High School is a Boys's only boarding high school, which is located in Kigumo near Kigumo Town, Kigumo Constituency, in Murang'a County; within the Central Region of Kenya. The school is classified as an Extra County School found in Murang'a County.

With the able stewardship of the school’s current Senior Principal, Mr. J.W. Waithaka, the Board of Management (BOM), the Parents Association (P.A.), the Teaching Staff, our industrious students, and non-teaching staff, the school continues to rise higher, operating under strict academic, moral and spiritual principles. The school’s motto; ’Strive to Victory’ is targeted to inject a self-success-driven attitude into the students, management and staff in our collective quest to academic success.


Besides being an academic giant, the School has continuously received regional, national and international recognition in various co-curriculum fields. The school is proud to note that it has produced quite a good number of very prominent personalities in the country.
